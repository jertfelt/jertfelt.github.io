Squidwardly Turkey Goo

You need these items:

  1. Cookies from Italy
    (preferably homebaked by someone's kind grandmother)
  2. Black radishes
  3. One turkey medium-sized
  4. One fresh octopus
  5. Makrut limes
    (or ordinary ones we are not that picky)
  6. Seasoning of your choice

You do it like this:

  • Toss the octopus several times until it stops moving
  • Fry the chicken until it stops moving
  • Eat some of the cookies
  • Boil one of the radishes
  • Put everything on a plate and pretend it's fancy dinner
  • Drink wine instead

Surprise, B!

You need this:

  1. 300g Spagetti (the cheap one from the homophobic Barilla)
  2. Cheap-ass candles
  3. Sponge cake (Here is the recipe on how to do that nicely)
  4. 200g tomato sauce
  5. ketchup
  6. A frenemy that you will serve this to

You do this:

  • Boil pastawater and then add the pasta with some salt, let it boil for 8-15 minutes depending on what Barilla corp-gorillas tells you to do. Or don't, be a rebel.
  • Cut the spongecake in half
  • Microwave the ketchup and the tomato sauce, you can add some herbs here if you like
  • Rinse the pasta, mix it with the tomatoketchup-sauce and then add it forcefully into the spongecake
  • Decorate with any amount of cheap-ass candles
  • We recommend serving this to someone special in your life. Can be your brother, your mother, anyone from your work (except your boss, or do it, it's your funeral and his birthday I guess) preferably anyone with gluten intolerance to spice things up between you guys.

C'est eNöff

You need this:

  1. A pig's foot*
  2. More black beets
  3. Condensed Milk
  4. 2 Jell-O Mix powder*
  5. Cheddar cheese
  6. Sardines
  7. Green olives
  8. Celery
  9. Lime
  10. Whipped Cream
  11. Peach
  12. Walnuts
  13. Food colouring: red and green

*If you prefer, you can actually get a whole pig and then boil its skin to create your own gelatine. Warning: it smells a lot.

You do this:

  • Boil the pig's foot, preferably somewhere else than your own living space.
  • Mix the condensed milk with lime, one of the Jell-O mixes and red color
  • Mix the other Jell-O mix with water and lime and green color
  • Put the chopped peaches (chopped in half, mind!) in a bowl and then pour down the jell-O.
  • Chop a lot of walnuts and cheddar cheese and put them in another bowl. Then pour the green Jell-O mix over them.
  • Put the Jell-O:s in a fridge for 12+ hours
  • When pig and Jell-O is done: flip over Jell-O:s over serving plates.
  • The red one will now be garnished with the prised pig's foot. And some whipped cream.
  • Garnish with some uncooked beets on the side, for the friend that insists that s-/he is vegetarian.
  • Light a candle to set the mood.
